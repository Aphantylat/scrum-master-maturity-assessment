What Kind of Scrum Master Are You?

Take this totally awesome assessment to find out your Scrum Master maturity level!

Being a Scrum Master is a journey of continuous growth and learning. This assessment, inspired by South Park's unique style, will help you understand where you are on this exciting path. But remember - it's not about reaching a specific destination; it's about the lessons you learn along the way!

Before you start, keep these important points in mind:

  • This assessment is a guide for personal development, not a strict evaluation
  • Focus on identifying areas for growth rather than achieving a specific level
  • Different situations may require different approaches - flexibility is key
  • Progress isn't always linear - you may show characteristics of multiple levels
  • Use your results to create a personal development plan

Remember: The goal isn't to race to the "Expert" level, but to continuously improve and provide the most value to your organization in your current context.
